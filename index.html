About Me

Matthew Wieciech is a skilled web content manager and coordinator who mainly focuses on front-end experiences. He is highly skilled in various content management systems including Wordpress, Hybris, Webflow, Wix, Squarespace, and proprietary enterprise-level systems. Matthew also has expertise in customisation of templatised content with modern front-end languages including HTML5, CSS3, and React.js. In addition, he has worked in system administration managing data in a linux framework and troubleshooting front-end CMS. Matthew has experience across multiple industries, including marketing, retail, and banking, having worked for Total Wine & More, Under Armour, and Capital One.

Experience Highlights:

  • Creates daily/weekly/monthly marketing content updates with traditional CMS.
  • Builds highly visible and revenue producing promotions on quick turnaround with custom and templatised content.
